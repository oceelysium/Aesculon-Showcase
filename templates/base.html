Welcome to Aesculon

Aesculon is a study tool built for ANU Year 2 Medicine students to practise SBA questions. It currently contains ~200 Block 4 and ~290 Block 5 questions, and around ~140 Year 1 questions (CVS and Endocrine for Mid-Year Exam).

Use Practice to start answering questions and Due Review for spaced repetition (this feature is not well implemented, sorry Anki lovers). Duel is a fun mode to play with classmates, make a custom lobby and challenge your friends!

Most questions are generated from lecture-aligned material, but due to constraints on AI context windows and my personal lack of time, you may find some content (perhaps 5-10%) that was not strictly covered in the supplied lectures. If a question feels outside the scope, please mark it as "Not learnt" so it can be manually reviewed and deleted if appropriate.
